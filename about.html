About Me

Hi! I'm Chandan Mishra, the creator of Explore Odyssey — a passion project that combines my love for technology and travel. This website is designed to help travelers discover new destinations and plan their journeys with ease.

I specialize in web development and have skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and backend technologies. I love building interactive and meaningful digital experiences.

Explore Odyssey is more than just a travel site — it's a platform to inspire adventures. I'm excited to continue developing it and adding new features to help fellow explorers.
